Metamask

Click here for Youtube tutorial
‍
1. Download Metamask 🦊 or use an existing wallet.
‍
Head to metamask.io and download their wallet to your phone or chrome/firefox browser. Please also add the BSC Smart Chain Network to your wallet, a useful tutorial can be found here: LINK

2. Go to PancakeSwap 🥞 (pancakeswap.finance).
PancakeSwap is where you'll be performing the swap of your BNB tokens to $WILLIE. You can use the link on our homepage to skip Number 3.

3. Get to the trade screen.
Click "Connect" at the top right of the screen to connect your Metamask or Trustwallet, and then navigate to "Trade" on the left sidebar.

4. Select the $WILLIE token 🐳.
Click on the "Select a Currency" button, and enter the $WILLIE token contract: LINK
‍
5. Adjust your slippage to 12%.
Click "Settings" at the top right, and adjust your slippage to 12% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is).

6. Welcome to the ocean.
Enter the amount you want to buy and swap away! Remember to have enough BNB for gas fees! ❤️ Remember you can chat with our community on our Telegram group.

Trust Wallet

Click here for Youtube Tutorial

You will need to add the Binance Smart Chain network to your wallet.

Download the Trust Wallet App on your Mobile Phone

If you are on IOS, head onto the App Store and search "Trust Wallet", it should be the first option with the shield logo. Once installed, you will need to enable the trust wallet browser on your IOS device by pasting this link (trust://browser_enable) on your safari browser. If you are on Android, head onto the Google Play Store and again search "Trust Wallet" and it should be the first option with the shield logo.


‍Buy and Send BNB to your Trust Wallet

You can buy Smart Chain BNB directly on Trust Wallet or you can buy it on an exchange like Binance and send it to your wallet address which can be found on the Trust Wallet app just mentioned. Press the receive button to automatically copy your BEP20 address; it should start with “0x”.

Go on PancakeSwap and Swap your Smart Chain BNB for WILLIE

‍ Click here to go directly to PancakeSwap or copy our contract address on BscScan here.

After you've copied the address, paste it into the search bar on PancakeSwap, selecting WILLIE from the list and setting the slippage to 11%. If you get an error, increase the slippage to 12% and if you continue to get errors, round your WILLIE amount to the nearest ten. (For example 534.23 WILLIE would round to 530 WILLIE)

Check you have got your WILLIE in your Wallet. If you cannot see WILLIE in your wallet, click manage in the top-right hand corner of the Trust Wallet app and insert the WILLIE address there. Congratulations, you are now a holder of WILLIE!
